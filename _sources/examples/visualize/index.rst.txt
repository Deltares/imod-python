Visualization
=============

imod contains a number of utilities to ease plotting of cross sections and maps
with `matplotlib`_, and 3D plots with `pyvista`_.

.. _matplotlib: https://matplotlib.org/
.. _pyvista: https://docs.pyvista.org/
